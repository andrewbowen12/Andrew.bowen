My Story

Why I Play

I picked up my first guitar as a teenager, mostly self-taught on YouTube videos and chord charts. When I was in Ghana, music became a bridge — I'd pull out a guitar at community gatherings and it didn't matter that we spoke different languages. There's something universal about a chord progression that no spreadsheet has ever achieved.

Now I play mostly fingerstyle and acoustic folk. It balances out the left-brain rigor of accounting — the same attention to detail that makes a good auditor makes a careful guitarist.

"Music gives a soul to the universe, wings to the mind, flight to the imagination, and life to everything." — Plato
